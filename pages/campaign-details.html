The Story

In the rural villages of Maharashtra, thousands of children are deprived of basic education due to lack of resources. Many families cannot afford school supplies, uniforms, or books, forcing children to drop out of school at an early age.

Our "Education for All" campaign aims to change this reality by providing comprehensive educational support to 500 children across 20 villages. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and build a better future.

What We're Doing

  • Providing school bags, books, and stationery to 500 children
  • Sponsoring school uniforms and shoes for underprivileged students
  • Setting up after-school tutoring programs in 20 villages
  • Creating small libraries with essential educational materials
  • Training local teachers with modern teaching methods
