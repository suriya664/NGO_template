Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 14th October 2025

At NGO Template Foundation, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you make a donation, volunteer, or register for events, we may collect:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number, address)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party gateways)
  • PAN number (for tax receipt generation under Indian Income Tax Act)
  • Demographic information (age, gender - optional)
  • Communication preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Cookies and usage data
  • Pages visited and time spent

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process donations and issue tax receipts (80G certificates)
  • Manage volunteer applications and event registrations
  • Send updates about our campaigns and impact (only if you opt-in)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure Payment Gateways (Razorpay, PayU) - we never store card details
  • Access Controls - limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Security Audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Data Backup and disaster recovery procedures

4. Payment Information

All payment transactions are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. We do not store your credit/debit card information on our servers. Payment data is encrypted and handled in accordance with PCI DSS standards.

5. Sharing of Information

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers (payment processors, email services) under strict confidentiality
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With your explicit consent

6. Your Rights

Under Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction - Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out - Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability - Receive your data in a structured format

7. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies - Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies - Help us understand website usage
  • Marketing Cookies - Used for targeted communications (with consent)

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for:

  • Donation records - 7 years (as per Indian Income Tax Act)
  • Volunteer information - Duration of volunteering + 2 years
  • Marketing data - Until you unsubscribe or request deletion

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored on servers located in India. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place as per applicable laws.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.
